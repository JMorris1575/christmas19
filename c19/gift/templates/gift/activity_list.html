Trivia Game

Compete with other family members for the best score in a Christmas Trivial game. You're on the honor system here. No fair asking Alexa or Siri or whatever other cybernetic voices there may be, and no looking things up in books or on the internet. Just trust your own mind.

What Is It?

Ever hear of a game called "Fictionary?" This is something like it. Given a picture of a not exactly everyday object, try to convince people that you know what it is. Then, seeing only the descriptions, not their authors, vote for what you think is the most believable one that is not your own. Finally the authors, the votes and the actual description of the object will all be revealed.

Recipe Game

Janet supplied some old recipes from Aunt Helen O'Brien that form the basis for this game. Based only on the ingredients, can you guess what Aunt Helen was making? After you've made your guesses you will be able to see scans of the original recipes. Perhaps you would like to try to make these things yourself.
